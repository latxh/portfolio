Verve (2020)

Font Design, University of Toronto

Verve is a serif typeface referencing Goudy and Times New Roman. Verve is intended to be used as a display typeface, inspired by the dramatic, yet graceful movement of ballet and swing dancing. Designed by Brian Latchman.
